How I Teach



Instruments and Styles

I graduated from the University of Alaska Anchorage with honors in 2012. There, I studied music education with an emphasis in classical guitar. I teach Jazz, Classical, Pop, Rock, and more.
In addition to Guitar I also teach Piano, Bass Guitar, Ukulele, and Violin/Fiddle.

Teaching Style

My philosophy in teaching music is to tailor each lesson to my student's wants and needs. We all learn our best when we are intrinsically motivated. When we are allowed to chase our unique interests, we progress much faster and have more fun doing it! Some students have a need to understand the theory behind every chord, while others want to just dive right in and play. Whatever your learning style, I will make sure that we are reinforcing good playing. My secret sauce is giving you what you want and supplementing it with what you need.
